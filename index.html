About Amnani Hamizah

Hi! I'm Nur Amnani Hamizah binti Yusrizal, originally from Johor Bahru, Johor. I used to study Fine Art at Sekolah Seni Malaysia Johor, where I developed a strong foundation in creativity and visual expression. Currently, I’m a Semester 2 student in the MC112 program (Diploma in Communication and Media Studies) at UiTM Alor Gajah, Melaka. It’s a whole new world of learning, and I'm enjoying every part of the journey especially getting to explore media, storytelling, and communication in different forms. I’ve always had a big love for K-pop and dancing, and I’m naturally drawn to anything involving arts and crafts. I'm creative by nature, and I enjoy making things with my hands, expressing myself through movement, and exploring new artistic ideas. People say I'm cheerful, bubbly, and love to talk, but I’m also an introvert who values deep connections and quiet time to reflect. I try to see the good in people, appreciate the small things, and stay grounded in everything I do.

My Hobbies

Pen Drawings

The delicate art of pen drawing allows me to capture the world in its purest form. Each stroke tells a story, building intricate patterns and textures. It easy and doesn't need fancy aquipment to start.

Fine Line Work Detailed Portraits Birds Studies

Resin Making

In the transparent depths of resin, I create miniature worlds suspended in time. Flowers, colors, and light interplay to form pieces that seem to glow from within, capturing nature's ephemeral beauty in eternal form.

Keychain Decorative Objects Nature Preservation

Bead Crafts

I enjoy beads making because it’s fun, creative, and relaxing. There's something really satisfying about turning tiny, colorful beads into beautiful bracelets, keychains, or accessories. It allows me to express myself and explore different patterns, shapes, and colors. Beads making also helps me stay focused and calm it’s like a little form of therapy. I love how I can turn my ideas into something real and wearable. Plus, it feels great when I finish a piece and can say, “I made this!” It’s a hobby that lets me combine patience, creativity, and joy and it always puts a smile on my face.

Intricate Patterns Custom Jewelry product

Painting

The velvety richness of gouache allows me to create paintings with an almost tangible quality. This medium bridges the gap between watercolor's fluidity and oil's opacity, perfect for capturing the soft, romantic scenes that inspire me.

Ghibli Art Stiil Life flora and fauna

Sculpting

Working with clay, wood and other materials, I bring three-dimensional life to my artistic vision. The tactile nature of sculpting allows me to explore form and texture in ways that two-dimensional art cannot achieve, creating pieces that invite touch and contemplation.
